About Me

"I can always cut something off, but I can't add it back on" — that's the cornerstone of both engineering and my life.

I'm a senior at West Windsor-Plainsboro High School South, planning to study Industrial Design or Engineering. I've completed NJIT's Summer Architecture and Portfolio Program and work as a lifeguard and swim instructor. I'm also an Eagle Scout and a maker who believes learning happens best when you build.
